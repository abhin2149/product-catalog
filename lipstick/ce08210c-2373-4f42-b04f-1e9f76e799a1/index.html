Air Kiss
Air Kiss (010)
  • Description
  • Details
  • How To Use It
30% lighter than your average lipstick. ColorStay Matte Lite Crayon™ is so lightweight you’ll forget it’s even on. Experience saturated color in a comfortable, non-drying matte formula infused with antioxidant-rich Mango Seed Oil to help replenish lips. In 12 bold yet wearable shades for color play all day.
  • Made with featherlight nano-pigments for a feel that’s 30% lighter than your average lipstick
  • Features an ultra-precise tip and built-in sharpener, so it’s easier to apply on the fly
  • Smudge-proof, flake-proof and water-resistant
  • No feathering, bleeding or fading
  • No parabens or mineral oil
 
  • Trace your lips with Matte Lite Crayon™ (use the crayon tip).
  • Fill in your lips and you’re all set. Make sure to keep your pencil sharpened with the built-in sharpener to make application a breeze every time.
  • Pro Tip: Pair two Matte Lite Crayon™shades to create a bold ombré look. Try Megan’s fave combo: On Cloud Wine as a lip liner and Air Kiss to fill in your lips.
