    Brass Off Shampoo

    Blue toning shampoo to neutralize brassy tones in dark blonde and light brunette hair.
    Share this product
    Find a Salon
    Details + −
    Matrix Brass Off is a professional salon clarifying shampoo that neutralizes brassy tones while refreshing your hair with cool tones. Best for brunettes who lighten their hair, this shampoo deposits balancing blue-violet pigments to reduce brassy tones.


    KEY BENEFITS


    Deposits balancing blue-violet pigments to reduce brassy tones
    Can be left on hair for 2-3 minutes for added toning
    Suitable for color treated and natural hair
    How To Use + −

    Use as a complete system with Brass off Conditioner and mask. Apply to wet hair, massage into a lather and rinse. Follow with Conditioner.
