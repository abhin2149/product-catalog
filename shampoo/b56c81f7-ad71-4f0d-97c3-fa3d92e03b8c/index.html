PRODUCT DETAILS
EverPure Sulfate-Free Moisture Shampoo deeply replenishes dry hair with 2X more moisture than non-conditioning shampoo. It’s gentle on color-treated hair and keeps color pure for up to 4 weeks. Plus, the rosemary-infused, sulfate-free formula:
  • Doesn’t contain harsh salts or surfactants
  • Doesn’t dull or damage hair
  • Leaves hair soft with luminous shine
  • Is gluten-free and mineral oil free


Packaging may vary*
WHO SHOULD USE
EverPure Sulfate-Free Moisture Shampoo is suitable for all hair types. You should especially reach for this color-protection shampoo if you have:
  • Dry hair
  • Dull hair
  • Color-treated hair
  • Hair that needs a boost of moisture
WHEN TO USE
EverPure Sulfate-Free Moisture Shampoo is safe to use daily.
HOW TO USE
We know hair washing is pretty simple, right? Well, just in case you need a refresher, follow these steps:

Step 1. Apply shampoo to wet hair.

Step 2. Massage gently into a thick lather.

Step 3. Rinse thoroughly. Then follow with EverPure Sulfate-Free Moisture Conditioner.
INGREDIENTS

  • Rosemary

Rosemary or Rosmarinus officinalis L. is an evergreen herb known for its distinct fragrance and its enhancement in blood flow, making it a viable ingredient for hair serums and oils. Studies have shown that rosemary oil helps promote hair growth — in some cases just as effectively as minoxidil. It also has proven antibacterial and antioxidant benefits, which explains why many people keep rosemary oil in the home at all times.
